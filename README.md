Ow! Privacy Policy
Information We Collect
Ow! does not collect, store, or share any personal information. This includes IP addresses, device identifiers, usernames, or any data you provide in the app.
No usage data, entry data, or other information is collected, whether you are subscribed or not. All functionality works entirely on your device.
Children’s Privacy
Ow! is intended for users aged 13 and up. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users under 18 should obtain parental consent before using the app.
Your Choices and Controls
Because no information is collected, there are no permissions to manage, and no data to delete.
Security
All data remains on your device. No personal information is transmitted or stored externally.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this policy to reflect changes in app features or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
